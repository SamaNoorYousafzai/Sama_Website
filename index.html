Advancing Regenerative Medicine and Placenta-Derived Healthcare Innovation Through Science, Ethics, and Partnership.

Strategic Alliance for Medical Access (SAMA) is a Canadian nonprofit organization dedicated to expanding access to regenerative medicine, advanced wound care, and healthcare innovation through ethical partnerships, research, education, and humanitarian collaboration for underserved communities.

Collaborate With SAMA Learn More

What We Do

SAMA connects healthcare professionals, researchers, organizations, and communities to support ethical medical innovation and improve access to life-changing healthcare solutions.

Regenerative Medicine: Supporting ethical research and partnerships in emerging regenerative healthcare solutions.
Medical Access: Helping underserved communities connect with advanced healthcare opportunities.
Collaboration: Building partnerships with healthcare providers, researchers, and humanitarian organizations.

Mission

To expand equitable access to regenerative medicine and healthcare innovation through ethical practices, scientific collaboration, education, and community-centered initiatives.

Focus Areas

Regenerative medicine, ethical placental tissue donation awareness, advanced wound care, healthcare education, research partnerships, and humanitarian medical access.

Vision

A world where every patient, regardless of location or income, has access to safe, ethical, and innovative regenerative healthcare solutions.
